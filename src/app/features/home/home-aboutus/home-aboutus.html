About Us

At PlayMerce, we bring sports and technology together to create a one-stop platform for athletes, fans, and enthusiasts. From booking your favorite turf to joining tournaments and shopping sports gear online — PlayMerce has it all.

Our mission is to make sports more accessible, enjoyable, and connected. Whether you’re a casual player, a professional athlete, or a fan, PlayMerce empowers you with tools to book, shop, and participate in the world of sports with ease.

Aniket Hiraskar

Founder & CEO

Aniket Hiraskar founded PlayMerce with a vision to transform sports engagement. Blending e-commerce, turf booking, and tournament management, he aims to build India’s leading digital sports hub where players, teams, and fans come together seamlessly.
